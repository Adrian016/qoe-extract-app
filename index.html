QoE Extract

A private, self-hosted tool for extracting financial reports from QuickBooks Online for buy-side Quality of Earnings analysis.

About

QoE Extract is a single-user command-line utility used to pull historical accounting reports (AR aging, P&L, balance sheet, etc.) from a QuickBooks Online company that the operator has been authorized to access. The tool runs locally on the operator's own computer; no data is transmitted to any third-party server.

Connect

To connect the tool to a QuickBooks Online company, run the CLI locally and follow the in-terminal instructions. You will be redirected to Intuit's authorization page to log in and select the company you want to grant read access to.

Reconnect

If access tokens expire or are revoked, rerun the CLI's authorization command to obtain fresh credentials. Reconnection follows the same flow as the initial connection.

Disconnect

To disconnect QoE Extract from your QuickBooks Online company at any time, log in to QuickBooks Online and visit Apps → My Apps, locate this application in the list, and choose Disconnect. You may also delete the locally stored token file on the machine running the tool.
