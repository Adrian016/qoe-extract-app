Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-29

This Privacy Policy describes how QoE Extract ("the Application") handles information accessed from QuickBooks Online ("QBO") when the Application is authorized by a QBO account holder.

1. Information accessed

When authorized, the Application accesses accounting data from the connected QBO company, including but not limited to: customer lists, vendor lists, invoices, bills, payments, general ledger entries, financial statements, and aging reports. Access is read-only. The Application does not access banking credentials, payroll personally identifiable information beyond what appears in standard accounting reports, or QBO login credentials.

2. How information is used

Accessed data is used solely for the operator's internal financial analysis, due diligence, and reporting purposes. Data is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for marketing.

3. Storage and security

The Application is operated locally on the operator's own computer. Any data retrieved from QBO is stored on that local machine in files controlled by the operator. OAuth tokens are stored locally and are not transmitted to any third-party server. The operator is responsible for the security of the device on which the Application runs.

4. Data sharing

Data accessed by the Application is not shared with any third party. Data may be reviewed by parties involved in the operator's internal due diligence engagement (for example, the operator's accountant, attorney, or financing source) under the operator's direct control.

5. Retention and deletion

Retrieved data and OAuth tokens persist on the operator's local machine until the operator deletes them. To revoke the Application's access to a QBO company, log in to QuickBooks Online, navigate to Apps → My Apps, locate this application, and choose Disconnect.

6. Children's privacy

The Application is not directed to, and does not knowingly collect information from, children under 13.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will be revised when changes are made.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the operator listed in the Intuit app registration.
